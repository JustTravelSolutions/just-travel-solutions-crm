About Us

The story behind Just Travel Solutions CRM

Just Travel Solutions CRM was created from real-world frustration. As a travel agency owner, managing JTS Vacations meant using too many systems just to keep things running smoothly.

We juggled tools for client management, booking forms, payments, commission tracking, and emails. It worked for a while, but it wasn't sustainable. Costs piled up. Processes became messy. Nothing synced.

In 2025, we expanded into a host agency and started bringing on agents. That growth revealed an even greater need for a system built specifically for the travel business.

So we built our own.

Just Travel Solutions CRM is the all-in-one platform we needed and now share with other agencies ready to streamline and grow.

Our Mission

To simplify travel agency operations with an all-in-one CRM that's practical, affordable, and built to scale.

Our Vision

To be the go-to CRM for travel professionals who want to grow smarter, not harder.

Who We Are

We are travel pros and problem solvers. Our founder, Shamica Davis, has been running JTS Vacations since 2014. The agency continues to curate custom group trips and immersive travel experiences.

Our leadership team understands what it really takes to build and scale a travel business because we've lived it.
