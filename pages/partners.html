Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our partner program

Requirements vary by partnership type, but generally include having an established business in the travel industry or related technology sector, a commitment to promoting Just Travel Solutions CRM, and alignment with our company values and quality standards.

The typical application process takes 1-2 weeks from initial submission to partnership approval. This includes the initial application review, consultation call, and agreement signing. The onboarding process may take an additional 1-2 weeks depending on the partnership type.

Reseller partners typically earn 20-30% commission on initial subscription sales and 15-20% on renewals. Commission rates may vary based on partnership tier, sales volume, and other factors. Detailed commission structures are provided during the partnership consultation.

Technology partners receive access to our API documentation, developer sandbox environment, integration guides, and technical support. We also provide regular updates on our product roadmap and opportunities to participate in beta testing of new features.

There is no cost to apply or join our standard partner program. However, certain partnership tiers or specialized integrations may require investment in development resources or certification programs. These details are discussed during the partnership consultation.
