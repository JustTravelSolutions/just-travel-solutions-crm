Introduction to Just Travel Solutions CRM

Last updated: April 10, 2025 By: Documentation Team

Welcome to the Just Travel Solutions CRM documentation. This comprehensive guide will help you understand and utilize all the features of our travel agency management platform.

What is Just Travel Solutions CRM?

Just Travel Solutions CRM is an all-in-one management system designed specifically for travel agencies. It combines client management, booking tracking, commission management, itinerary building, marketing tools, and more into a single, integrated platform.

Key Benefits

  • Streamline your agency operations with integrated tools
  • Increase revenue by tracking and recovering commissions
  • Enhance client satisfaction with professional itineraries and communication
  • Grow your business with powerful marketing and pipeline management

Core Modules

Just Travel Solutions CRM consists of several integrated modules, each designed to address specific aspects of travel agency management:

Client Management

Store comprehensive client profiles including contact information, travel preferences, important dates, and communication history. Segment clients for targeted marketing campaigns.

Learn more

Booking System

Track all bookings and reservations in one place. Store supplier information, booking details, payment status, and related documents for easy access.

Learn more

Commission Tracking

Monitor commissions from all suppliers with detailed reporting on pending, invoiced, and paid commissions. Set up automated reminders for outstanding commissions.

Learn more

Itinerary Builder

Create beautiful, interactive itineraries for your clients. Import content from suppliers and customize with your branding for a professional presentation.

Learn more

Getting Started

To begin using Just Travel Solutions CRM, follow these steps:

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for a subscription plan that meets your agency's needs. You'll receive login credentials via email.

2

Set Up Your Agency Profile

Configure your agency details, branding elements, and user accounts for your team members.

3

Import Your Data

Use our import tools to migrate your existing client database, bookings, and supplier information.

4

Customize Your Workflow

Configure your sales pipeline stages, commission rules, and other settings to match your agency's processes.
